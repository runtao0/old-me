My 3D Life

Github Repo

A 3D version of Conway's Game of Life in the form of Three.js cubes on a 100 X 100 miniverse. It utilizes raycasting and trackballer capabilities to create a voxel painter and mouse viewing controls. Watch patterns grow into tree/coral structures.
